Project Brief

  • Vintage Vibes Café Logo Design
  • Client: Vintage Vibes Café

Project Overview:

Vintage Vibes Café, a new coffee shop, aimed to create a logo that encapsulates its unique blend of nostalgic charm and modern comfort. The goal was to design a logo that resonates with young professionals, students, and coffee enthusiasts who appreciate a vintage aesthetic.

Design Requirements:

  • Style: Vintage with a modern twist
  • Colors: Earthy tones (e.g., browns, creams)
  • Elements: Incorporate a vintage camera and a coffee cup
  • Typography: Retro-inspired fonts

Deliverables:

  • Primary logo
  • Secondary logo (simplified version)
  • Black and white version

Outcome:

The final logo features a stylized steaming mug with an intricate design of a vintage camera inside, using a monochromatic brown color scheme. This design successfully combines the warmth of a traditional coffee mug with the rustic charm of a vintage camera, perfectly capturing the essence of Vintage Vibes Café.
